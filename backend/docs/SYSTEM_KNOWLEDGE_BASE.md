# Esthington CRM: Staff Operations Handbook

Welcome to the Esthington CRM Knowledge Base. This document is designed to guide staff on how to use the CRM efficiently, categorized strictly by your assigned role. 

---

## 1. Marketers & Business Development Managers (BDMs)

### How to Register a New Lead
1. Navigate to the **Leads** section on your dashboard.
2. Click the **Register Lead** button.
3. Fill in the required client information (Name, Email, Phone, Address, Interest).
4. Save. The lead is now assigned to you and you can track its conversion status.

### How to Pitch Properties
- Head to the **Estates** or **Properties** tab to view the latest available plots.
- Remember: Emphasize urgency ("Real estate appreciates quickly") and our 100% verified C of O titles.
- For clients hesitant about the price, always mention the flexible 3 to 6-month installment plans requiring only a 30% initial deposit.

### How to Apply a Discount Code
1. Once a lead is ready to buy, click **Sell Plot** or **Record Sale**.
2. Select the Plot and the Lead.
3. Enter the promotional discount code in the **Discount Code** field.
4. The system will automatically validate the code (checking expiration and limits) and apply the correct flat or percentage discount to the final price.

### How to Close a Sale
1. Navigate to the Lead profile or Properties section.
2. Select **Record Sale**.
3. Choose the Lead and the specific plot they are purchasing.
4. If it is a Corner Piece, remind them of the 20% faster appreciation rate to justify the surcharge.
5. Review the terms, ensure they accept, and submit the sale. The accountant will be notified to confirm payment.

## 2. Business Development Directors (BDD)

### How to Manage Marketing Teams
1. Access the **Analytics** or **Team Management** dashboard.
2. You can view real-time conversion rates, total leads registered, and sales closed by your downlines.
3. Use this data to coach underperforming marketers and approve team requisitions.

---

## 3. Site Experts / Engineers

### How to Manage Plot Allocations and Inspections
1. Log into your dashboard and navigate to the **Allocations** or **Inspections** tab.
2. Review the list of clients scheduled for site visits.
3. After an inspection or upon final payment, you will manage the physical allocation of the specific plot numbers (e.g., Corner Pieces vs. Standard Plots) on the digital estate map.

---

## 4. Customer Care

### How to Manage Helpdesk Tickets
1. Navigate to the **Helpdesk** or **Tickets** section.
2. When a client calls or emails with an issue, click **New Ticket**.
3. Select the relevant category (e.g., Payment Issue, Document Delay, General Inquiry) and assign priority.
4. As you work on the issue, update the ticket status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved) so management and the client stay informed.

---

## 5. Accountants & Treasury

### How to Review and Approve Payment Receipts
1. Navigate to the **Sales & Payments** or **Treasury** dashboard.
2. Look for sales with a `PENDING` payment status.
3. Open the sale record, verify the physical bank alert or proof of payment uploaded by the client/marketer.
4. Once verified, click **Approve Payment**. This will automatically generate the client's official receipt and trigger the Offer Letter generation.

### How to Process Refunds
1. If a refund is requested and approved by management, navigate to the **Refunds** module.
2. Locate the client's transaction.
3. Initiate the refund workflow, attaching the necessary approval memos, and adjust the financial records accordingly.

### How to Approve Fund Requisitions
1. Go to the **Requisitions** tab.
2. Review fund requests from staff (e.g., marketers requesting site-visit fuel allowance).
3. If the request meets budget criteria, you (or the appropriate Director) can approve it for disbursement.

---

## 6. Branch Admins & HR

### How to Handle Leave Requests
1. Navigate to the **Leave Approvals** section.
2. Review pending requests (Annual, Sick, Casual, Maternity, etc.).
3. Approve or Decline based on the staff member's available leave days and branch coverage needs.

### How to Manage Local Branch Operations
1. Use the **Branch Staff** directory to onboard new hires.
2. Assign them the correct roles (Marketer, Customer Care, etc.) and assign them to your specific branch.

---

## 7. Managing Directors & Group Managing Directors (GMDs)

### How to Broadcast Executive Memos
1. Navigate to the **Executive Memos** tab.
2. Click **Compose Memo** / **New Executive Memo**.
3. **If you are an MD:** You will select recipients based on roles within your company.
4. **If you are a GMD:** You will see a special **Branch Filter** at the top. You can select "All Branches" or target a specific branch (e.g., Utako Branch) before filtering by role (e.g., Marketers) and utilizing the dynamic search bar.
5. Write your directive and hit Send. Standard staff will only see an "Acknowledge" button, preventing unnecessary declines of executive orders.

### How to View High-Level Analytics
1. The **Overview** dashboard aggregates sales, revenue, and active leads across your purview.
2. GMDs can view enterprise-wide metrics spanning all branches and companies.

---

## 8. System Admin / ICT

### How to Resolve System Errors and Manage Roles
1. Navigate to the **Settings** or **Admin Panel**.
2. From here, you can reset passwords, adjust user roles, and manage global system configurations.

### How to Record Legacy Sales
1. Navigate to the **Legacy Sales** module.
2. This is used strictly for migrating old, offline sales into the new CRM.
3. Fill out the form with historical data (past dates, old plot numbers, previous amounts paid).
4. Submitting a legacy sale bypasses the standard immediate alerts and email triggers, ensuring that old clients don't suddenly receive confusing "New Sale" notifications today.
